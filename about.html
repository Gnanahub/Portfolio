About

I'm Sai. A Data Scientist, maker and problem solver.

The convergence of data science and technology has always intrigued me, leading me to explore various tools and techniques like Python, Pandas, SQL, and advanced analytics. From basic data analysis to advanced machine learning, my journey parallels the evolution from Microsoft Paint to sophisticated data science methodologies.

By 2023, I've gained expertise in data visualization with Tableau and Power BI, Market research, Azure Data Factory, Predictive modeling, and specialties like Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Computer Vision. What excites me about being a Data Scientist is the opportunity to solve real-world problems and drive insights from data, akin to designing products with purpose.

My skill set includes leading cross-functional teams, project management, and ethical considerations in data practices. I leverage platforms like GCPs, Salesforce, and JIRA to manage data pipelines, develop machine learning models, and create impactful data visualizations. My approach revolves around understanding customer needs, delivering value quickly, and continuous improvement through iterative processes.

This is one of my favourite quotes:

“Don't mistake speed for effectiveness; it's better to strike with purpose than to strike hastily”
This sentiment strikes a chord within me because I have no inclination to craft mere aesthetics for their own sake. While some revel in that pursuit, it doesn't align with my aspirations. My sole ambition is to conceive, design, or construct ideas and products that possess genuine substance, that hold significance, and that leave a meaningful imprint on the world.
